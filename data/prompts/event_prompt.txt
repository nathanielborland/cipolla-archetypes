- Intelligent: benefits both self and others (+,+)
- Bandit: benefits self while harming others (+,-)
- Helpless: harms self while benefiting others (-,+)

Continuous impact scoring:
- Self-impact score (S):

  50 = neutral (no clear gain or loss)

- Other-impact score (O):
